Product Introduction

Pure white garlic is the premium choice for buyers who value clean, bright appearance and uniform bulb presentation. Unlike purple skin varieties, pure white garlic features entirely white outer skins — a visual characteristic highly prized in Middle Eastern, European, and upscale retail markets worldwide.

Cultivated in Shandong's prime garlic-growing region, our pure white garlic develops dense, plump cloves with a balanced flavor profile — aromatic and flavorful without overwhelming pungency. The tight white skin provides excellent natural protection, extending shelf life and maintaining product integrity throughout long-distance shipping.

Every shipment undergoes rigorous quality control: bulbs are hand-sorted by diameter, inspected for skin integrity, and checked for sprout, moisture, and root specifications. Our cold-storage facilities maintain product freshness from the June harvest through March, ensuring year-round availability for our global customer base.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pure white garlic

Why is pure white garlic preferred in the Middle East?
Middle Eastern buyers favor pure white garlic for its clean, bright appearance, which is considered premium quality. The uniform white skin signals freshness and high-grade product, making it ideal for retail display and gifting.
What sizes are most popular for pure white garlic export?
The most requested sizes for pure white garlic are 5.5cm and 6.0cm+, as Middle Eastern and European markets prefer larger, visually uniform bulbs. However, all five sizes (4.5cm-6.5cm+) are available.
Do you offer private label packaging for pure white garlic?
Yes. We provide custom packaging including branded carton printing, private label stickers, and retail-ready pre-packs. Minimum order quantities apply for custom packaging. Contact us with your branding requirements.
What quality standards does your pure white garlic meet?
Our pure white garlic meets international export standards: moisture below 7%, sprout rate below 1%, no mold or pesticide residue. We provide Phytosanitary Certificates, GlobalG.A.P., and HACCP compliance documentation with every shipment.
