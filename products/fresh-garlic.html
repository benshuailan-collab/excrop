Fresh Garlic Supplier China

ExCrop is a premium garlic exporter based in Shandong, China — the heart of the world's largest garlic-producing region. We supply Normal White Garlic and Pure White Garlic in sizes ranging from 4.5cm to 6.5cm+, all compliant with EU MRL and FDA pesticide residue standards. Every shipment is backed by GLOBALG.A.P. and HACCP certifications, with full phytosanitary inspection by China Customs. Product Introduction

ExCrop fresh garlic is sourced directly from Shandong province, one of China's most important garlic-producing regions. Our garlic is known for its firm texture, plump cloves, tight outer skin, and characteristic pungent aroma — qualities that make it highly sought after by importers and distributors worldwide.

We offer both purple skin garlic and pure white garlic varieties, giving buyers flexibility to match their market's preferences. Each batch is carefully sorted by size, inspected for quality, and packed under strict hygiene standards. From field to container, we control every step of the supply chain to ensure freshness, consistency, and compliance with international phytosanitary requirements.

With cold-storage facilities supporting year-round supply and a strategic location just 500 kilometers from Qingdao Port, ExCrop delivers reliable, on-time shipments to customers across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa, South America, and Europe.

Frequently Asked Questions

What B2B buyers and importers need to know

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for garlic export?
The MOQ is one 40-foot reefer container (28–30 MT for Normal White Garlic, 26–28 MT for Pure White Garlic). This minimum ensures cost-efficient freight rates and optimal container utilization. For trial orders, we can arrange LCL (Less than Container Load) shipments at an adjusted unit price, or mix different garlic varieties within one container.
How do you maintain garlic freshness during ocean freight?
Shipments are maintained at −2°C to −3°C with 60–70% relative humidity throughout transit. This temperature range keeps garlic in a dormant state, preventing sprouting and mold growth during voyages of 15–35 days. All 40' reefer containers are pre-cooled before loading and equipped with continuous temperature monitoring devices.
What certifications and phytosanitary documents do you provide?
Every shipment includes a Phytosanitary Certificate issued by China Customs, plus GLOBALG.A.P. and HACCP certifications. Our garlic complies with EU MRL (Maximum Residue Limits) and FDA pesticide residue standards. Third-party lab test reports — including 468-parameter pesticide screening — are available upon request for each crop season.
What are your accepted payment terms?
We accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer) and L/C (Letter of Credit). Standard T/T terms are 30% advance payment with 70% against the copy of Bill of Lading. For L/C, we accept irrevocable L/C at sight from top-tier banks. Payment terms are negotiable based on order volume and long-term partnership agreements — we are flexible to accommodate your procurement workflow.
What is the typical lead time from order confirmation to vessel departure?
Lead time is 7–15 days from order confirmation to vessel departure, depending on season and order volume. During peak harvest season (June–August), lead time may extend to 15–20 days due to high demand. We provide real-time shipment tracking and documentation updates at every stage: processing, packing, container loading, customs clearance, and vessel ETD/ETA.
