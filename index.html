FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about importing garlic from China. Can't find an answer? Contact us directly.

Excrop exports two main varieties: Normal White Garlic and Pure White Garlic, both sourced directly from Shandong Province, China. Sizes range from 4.5cm to 6.0cm.

The minimum order quantity is 1 container (approximately 28 tons). We can arrange full container loads (FCL) for both 20ft and 40ft containers. A 40ft container holds approximately 28-30 tons.

We export to 30+ countries across the Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia, South America, Europe, and South Asia. Key markets include UAE, Saudi Arabia, Russia, Brazil, Nigeria, Indonesia, and more.

We offer 10kg mesh bags, 20kg cartons, 20kg mesh bags, and custom packaging solutions. We also provide private label packaging with your brand upon request.

Every shipment includes: Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, Health Certificate, Packing List, Commercial Invoice, and Bill of Lading. Additional documents can be arranged per destination country requirements.

Lead time is 7-15 days from order confirmation to vessel departure from Qingdao Port. Sea freight transit time depends on destination: Middle East ~20-25 days, Southeast Asia ~7-15 days, Europe ~30-40 days, South America ~35-45 days.
