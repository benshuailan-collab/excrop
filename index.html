Quality Assurance

From Farm to Port, Quality at Every Step

01

Farm Selection

Partner farms in Shandong's best garlic zones. Soil quality, planting methods, and harvest timing are strictly controlled.

02

Size Grading

Each bulb is measured and sorted into precise diameter grades: 4.5, 5.0, 5.5, 6.0, 6.5cm+. No mixed sizes in any shipment.

03

Quality Inspection

Moisture, mold, sprouting, and damage checks. Only bulbs passing all criteria proceed to packing.

04

Cold Chain Storage

Temperature-controlled warehouses maintain freshness. Garlic stays in optimal condition until vessel loading.

05

Export Documentation

Phytosanitary certificates, health certificates, COA, and all required customs paperwork prepared for each destination.
